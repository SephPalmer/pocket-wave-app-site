1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how I, as the independent developer of Pocket Wave ("App") operating under the name Metaverse Experiments, collect, use, and protect information when you use the App available on the Google Play Store.

As an indie developer based in the United Kingdom, I'm committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about my data practices. The App does not require user registration or accounts. I do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details. I only collect minimal analytics data to improve the App experience.

2. Information I Collect

I collect only minimal, non-personal information necessary for App functionality and improvement:

2.1 Analytics and Usage Information

I automatically collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics:

  • App features accessed and frequency of use
  • Session duration and timestamps
  • In-app navigation paths and feature interactions
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • General usage patterns and statistics

2.2 Device Information

I collect basic device information to optimize App performance:

  • Device manufacturer, model, and operating system version
  • Android version and App version
  • Screen resolution and device capabilities
  • General location (country/city level) derived from IP address
  • Time zone and language settings
  • Network type (WiFi/mobile) without identifying specific networks

2.3 Audio and Media Access

With your explicit permission through Android system prompts:

  • Storage Access: To read and process audio files you select from your device
  • Microphone Access: For recording features within the App

Important: Audio files and recordings are processed locally on your device and are NEVER uploaded to my servers or any third-party servers

2.4 Identifiers

  • Android Advertising ID: Used for analytics purposes (you can reset or opt-out via Android settings)
  • Installation ID: A random ID generated upon App installation to identify unique installations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

As a UK-based developer, I process information under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) based on:

3.1 Legitimate Interests

  • Analytics to improve App functionality and user experience
  • Crash reporting to maintain App stability
  • Performance monitoring to optimize the App
  • Security monitoring to prevent abuse

3.2 Consent

  • Optional permissions (microphone, storage)
  • Use of advertising ID for analytics

4. How I Use Information

I use the collected anonymous information solely for:

  • App Improvement: Understanding which features are used most to prioritize development
  • Performance Optimization: Identifying and fixing crashes, bugs, and performance issues
  • User Experience: Analyzing usage patterns to improve interface and functionality
  • Technical Support: Diagnosing technical issues
  • Security: Detecting and preventing abuse or unauthorized use
  • Statistical Analysis: Creating aggregated reports about App usage

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

I do not sell, trade, or rent any user information. The minimal data collected may be shared only with:

5.1 Analytics Services

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): For usage analysis and crash reporting
  • Data is processed in accordance with Google's privacy practices
  • Google may process data in the United States and other countries

5.2 Legal Requirements

I may disclose information if required by UK law or other applicable laws to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate potential violations of the App's Terms of Service

5.3 App Transfer

In the unlikely event that Pocket Wave is sold or transferred to another developer or company, analytics data may be transferred to the new owner. Users will be notified via App update notes.

5.4 Aggregated Information

Completely anonymized, aggregated statistics that cannot identify any individual user may be shared publicly.

6. Data Security

I implement appropriate security measures to protect collected information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses encryption
  • Anonymization: No personal identifiers are collected
  • Access Controls: Only I have access to analytics dashboards
  • Third-Party Security: I use only reputable analytics providers with strong security practices
  • Local Processing: Audio/media files are processed on your device, not on external servers

7. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained as follows:

  • Firebase Analytics: Please refer to https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy for retention periods
  • Crash Reports: Please refer to https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy for retention periods
  • Aggregated Statistics: May be retained indefinitely in anonymous form

When you uninstall the App, all local data is immediately deleted from your device. Analytics data cannot be linked back to you as an individual.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Universal Controls

All users can:

  • Opt-out of Analytics:
    • Android Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • Reset Advertising ID in the same settings menu
  • Revoke Permissions: Android Settings > Apps > Pocket Wave > Permissions
  • Uninstall: Removing the App deletes all local data from your device

8.2 UK GDPR Rights

As the App doesn't collect personal information that can identify you, traditional GDPR rights (access, correction, deletion) don't apply to identified individuals. However, you can:

  • Object to analytics processing via opt-out mechanisms above
  • Lodge complaints with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you have privacy concerns
  • Contact me with any privacy questions

8.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

Under CCPA, since I don't collect personal information:

  • I don't sell personal information
  • I can't identify specific users to fulfill individual data requests
  • You can opt-out of analytics as described above

8.4 Other Jurisdictions

Users in other regions have similar controls through Android system settings.

9. Android Permissions

The App may request the following permissions through standard Android system prompts:

  • Storage/Media Access: To read audio files you select
  • Microphone: For recording features (if applicable)
  • Internet: For analytics data transmission

Denying optional permissions may limit some features but won't affect core functionality.

10. Third-Party Services

I use the following third-party service:

Firebase Analytics (Google)

  • Purpose: Anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting
  • Data Collected: Usage patterns, device info, crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

11. Children's Privacy

Pocket Wave does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since I don't collect personal information:

  • No parental consent is required
  • Parents concerned about their child's privacy can uninstall the App
  • The App's analytics are completely anonymous
  • The App is suitable for all ages

12. International Data Transfers

As a UK-based developer, anonymous analytics data may be processed in:

  • United Kingdom - Where I am based
  • United States - Google Firebase servers
  • Other countries - Where Google operates data centers

Since no personal information is collected, cross-border data transfer regulations have minimal application. Google handles data in accordance with their privacy policies and applicable laws.
